Reindeer/Elf Biographies
Dasher one of the fastest reindeer in Santa’s herd. He’s always ready to dash out the door.
Dancer Dancer is a reindeer with a unique personality. He’s completely extroverted. When he’s not helping Santa, he’s having dance parties.
Prancer Prancer loves the other reindeer and would do anything for them.
Vixen Vixen is the comedic reindeer known for lots of magic tricks. The other reindeer often get slightly annoyed with his ability to make things disappear and reappear.
Comet Comet is out of this world—as his name implies. He’s quite handsome and is always smiling. He’s easy-going and loves to play ball with all the young fawns.
Cupid Cupid is an affectionate reindeer. She has Christmas reins decorated with red and green little heart-shaped bells. Her motto is “Spread the Love.”
Donner  he’s got a deep booming baritone voice.
Rudolph He suffered from low self-esteem because he had an odd red nose. It wasn’t until Santa recognized his rosy bulb as a beacon of light that he was ushered into his role as leader of the herd.
Blitzen He’s fast, playful, and like a bolt when it comes to helping Santa deliver his Christmas goodies
